About UrVote.Rocks

Revolutionizing how brands, events, and creators discover and promote AI-generated content through community-driven contests.

What is UrVote.Rocks?

UrVote.Rocks is the first dedicated platform for AI content contests, connecting creators with opportunities to showcase their work and get discovered by brands and audiences.

🎵

AI Music Contests

From Suno to Udio, showcase your AI-generated music in themed contests and get discovered by music lovers worldwide.

🎬

Video Campaigns

Submit promotional videos, AI-generated content, or creative campaigns for brands and events to discover.

🎨

Visual Art Contests

Share your AI-generated artwork, graphics, and designs in contests that celebrate creativity and innovation.

How It Works

1

Create Your Media Board

Brands, events, and creators pay a fee to create a custom contest board. Set your theme, rules, prizes, and contest duration.

2

Content Creators Submit

Artists, musicians, and creators submit their AI-generated content (music, videos, visuals) to your contest board.

3

Community Votes

The community votes on submissions in real-time. Watch as your contest gains engagement and discovers amazing talent.

4

Winners Emerge

The content with the most votes wins your contest. Connect with talented creators and discover fresh, innovative content.
