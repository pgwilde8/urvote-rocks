Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about UrVote.Rocks - the media board contest platform for AI content and creative campaigns.

1 Platform Basics

UrVote.Rocks is a media board contest platform where creators can submit AI-generated music, videos, and visuals to compete in contests. Media board owners create contests, users submit content, and the community votes to determine winners.

Media board owners pay a fee to create a contest board. Users then submit content (music, videos, or visuals) to that board. The community votes on submissions, and the content with the most votes wins the contest. Each media board can set its own rules, prizes, and contest duration.

You can submit three types of content:

  • Music: AI-generated songs, original compositions, covers (50MB max)
  • Videos: AI-generated videos, promotional content, campaign videos, creative content (100MB max)
  • Visuals: AI-generated images, artwork, graphics, designs (25MB max)

2 Content Submission

File size limits are:

  • Music: Maximum 50MB
  • Videos: Maximum 100MB
  • Visuals: Maximum 25MB

These limits ensure fast uploads and smooth voting experience for all users.

Supported formats include:

  • Music: MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A
  • Videos: MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM
  • Visuals: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP

  • Content you created yourself

Content is typically approved automatically upon submission. However, if there are any issues with your content (file format, size, or content violations), you'll receive a notification. Approved content immediately appears on the media board and is available for voting.

3 Voting & Contests

Voting is simple and fair:

  • Each user can vote once per content submission
  • Votes are counted in real-time
  • Content with the most votes wins
  • Voting is open to all registered users

The voting results are displayed on each media board, showing current standings and top performers.

No, you cannot vote for your own content. This ensures fair competition and prevents vote manipulation. The system automatically detects and prevents self-voting.

Winners are determined by the total number of votes received during the contest period. The content with the highest vote count at the end of the contest wins. In case of a tie, the media board owner may decide the winner or extend the contest.

Prizes are determined by each individual media board owner, not by UrVote.Rocks. Some contests may offer cash prizes, recognition, exposure, or other rewards. Check the specific media board details to see what prizes are available for each contest.

4 Pricing & Media Boards

Pricing varies based on the type and features of your media board. Contact us for current pricing information and to discuss your specific needs. We offer competitive rates for businesses, events, and creators looking to run contests.

Each media board includes:

  • Custom contest board with your branding
  • Content submission system for music, videos, and visuals
  • Voting system and real-time results
  • User management and content moderation
  • Analytics and contest insights
  • Technical support and maintenance

Contest duration is flexible and set by each media board owner. Typical contest periods range from:

  • Short contests: 1-7 days (for quick campaigns)
  • Standard contests: 2-4 weeks (most common)
  • Extended contests: 1-3 months (for major events)

You can set your own timeline when creating your media board.

5 Support & Contact

If you encounter inappropriate content, you can report it by:

  • Using the report button on the content
  • Contacting support directly
  • Emailing us with details about the issue

All reports are reviewed promptly, and inappropriate content is removed immediately.

We appreciate bug reports and feedback! If you encounter any technical issues:

  • Try refreshing the page first
  • Check if the issue persists in different browsers
  • Contact support with specific details about the problem
  • Include screenshots if possible

We'll investigate and fix issues as quickly as possible.

You can reach our support team through:

  • Contact Form: Use the contact page on our website
  • Email: support@urvote.rocks
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 24 hours

For urgent issues, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.
