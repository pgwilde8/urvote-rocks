Submit Your Song to UrVote.Rocks

Share your AI-generated music with the world and compete for recognition, prizes, and a spot on the leaderboard!

Why Submit?

  • • Global Exposure: Get your music heard by a worldwide audience.
  • • Fair Voting: Every song is judged by the community, not just a panel.
  • • Win Prizes: Top songs earn recognition and special rewards provided by the contest sponsor, not UrVote.Rocks.
  • • Artist Profile: Build your brand and connect your social links.
  • • Promote Each Song: Add unique links for every song you submit—share your website, Linktree, or social media with every entry!
  • • Free to Enter: No cost to submit your AI-generated track!

How It Works

  1. Login or Register for a free account.
  2. Complete Your Profile (add artist name and bio).
  3. Upload Your Song (add your song file and, optionally, a website, Linktree, or social media link for this song).
  4. Get Approved by our team for quality and compliance.
  5. Share & Compete—promote your entry and climb the leaderboard!

Ready to Get Started?

You must be logged in to submit a song. If you don’t have an account, registration is quick and free!
